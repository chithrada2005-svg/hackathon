About PeerLix

PeerLix is a real-time collaborative platform designed to help you master new skills by connecting instantly with global peers.

Built for learners, by learners. No more waiting for asynchronous help—get live interactive sessions when you need them most.

How It Works

🔍

1. Find Peers

Browse the global peer network on your dashboard. Filter users by their active status (Online, Live, Offline) and view their primary expertise.

🤝

2. Connect & Match

Click the info button on any peer card to see a detailed profile, including their extra skills and social links. Decide if they are the right match for your learning goals.

💬

3. Learn Live

Hit "Connect" to instantly jump into a secure, real-time chat room where you can collaborate, ask questions, and grow your skills together.
